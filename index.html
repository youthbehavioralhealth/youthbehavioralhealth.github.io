About YBH

Youth Behavioral Health, LLC provides evaluation and therapeutic services for children, adolescents, and young adults who are struggling with internalizing disorders, such as anxiety and depression, as well as externalizing disorders such as behavioral challenges and AttentionDeficit/Hyperactivity Disorder.


Specialized services are provided to address disordered eating problems such as Anorexia Nervosa and Bulimia and medical concerns (e.g., pain, headaches, chronic illness, insomnia, adherence issues). After a thorough evaluation, evidence-based treatment will be discussed with each patient to collaboratively tailor the treatment plan to address the areas of greatest concern.

Services

Therapy is offered for a wide range of concerns, such as:

  1. Anxiety/Phobias

  2. Attention Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder

  3. Trauma/Post Traumatic Stress Disorder

  4. Depression/Mood

  5. Adjustment Issues

  6. Behavioral Problems

  7. Peer Relationship Conflicts

Meet Dr. Hord

Dr. Hord conducts diagnostic evaluations and provides psychological and behavioral treatment for individuals with a broad range of emotional, behavioral, learning, and developmental challenges. She specializes in coaching parents to utilize behavioral techniques to address behavioral issues and disordered eating concerns. In addition, Dr. Hord has a particular interest in working with individuals with anxiety related disorders, self-regulation challenges, as well as adjusting to and adhering to medical diagnoses.


Dr. Hord has received specialized training in and is seeking certification in both Parent-Child Interaction Therapy (PCIT) and Family Based Treatment for Eating Disorders. She is also trained in providing a range of empirically-researched approaches including: Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT), Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT), and Motivational Interviewing.


Dr. Hord earned her Ph.D. at the University of Maine, with a specialization in Developmental Clinical Psychology. She completed her pre-doctoral internship at Indiana University School of Medicine, in Indianapolis, IN, followed by a two-year postdoctoral fellowship at the Mayo Clinic in Rochester, MN.


Dr. Hord has presented at dozens of workshops, seminars, conferences, and taught college curriculum for over two years. Please contact her office to check speaking availability.
